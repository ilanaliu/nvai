Neuro Veritas AI is a student-founded tech startup focused on developing AI-driven speech analysis and logic testing tools to detect early signs of dementia across multiple languages. By combining linguistic patterns and logic testing, the group aims to revolutionize early diagnosis and cognitive health monitoring through accessible, non-invasive technology.

Our Mission

At Neuro Veritas AI, our mission is to advance early detection of dementia through research in AI-driven speech analysis. We are dedicated to developing multilingual models that can recognize subtle shifts in tone and linguistic patterns associated with cognitive decline well before clinical symptoms become evident.
